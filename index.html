About Us

Clintel Consulting is a specialized pharmacometric consulting firm dedicated to empowering drug development programs through rigorous quantitative modeling. Our team of scientists and data scientists brings decades of experience in population pharmacokinetics, PK/PD modeling, exposure–response analyses, and clinical pharmacology strategy. We partner with pharmaceutical and biotech companies to transform complex data into actionable insights.

Whether you need support optimizing clinical trial designs, interpreting PK/PD relationships, or guiding dose selection, our proven expertise delivers clarity and confidence in critical decisions.

Our Services

Population PK

Robust population pharmacokinetic modeling to characterize variability and inform dose optimization.

Population PK/PD

Integrative PK/PD modeling to link exposure with pharmacodynamic response, enabling evidence‑based decision making.

Exposure–Response Modeling

Quantitative exposure‑response analyses to elucidate dose–efficacy and dose–toxicity relationships across development stages.

Disease Progression Models

Model disease trajectories and natural history to support clinical trial planning and endpoint selection.

Non‑Compartmental Analysis

Rigorous NCA for early assessment of pharmacokinetic properties with clear and comprehensive reporting.

Regulatory Strategy Support

Guidance on pharmacometric strategy to satisfy regulatory expectations and streamline submissions.
